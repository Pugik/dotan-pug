=== A crutch way to disable Glyph and Scanner.
	You need to set a huge time as below.

	In the addon_game_mode.lua file in the initialisation logic:
		GameRules:GetGameModeEntity():SetCustomGlyphCooldown(172800.0) 	== 48 hours
		GameRules:GetGameModeEntity():SetCustomScanCooldown(172800.0)	== 48 hours

	When used, it will write that the ability is still on cooldown.
